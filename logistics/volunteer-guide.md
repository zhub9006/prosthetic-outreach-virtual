# Volunteer Task Coordination Guide

## Task Categories

### 🔬 Research
- Search and summarize clinical trials for affordable prosthetics
- Review prosthetic technology developments
- Map trial locations to regional hubs

### 📦 Logistics
- Plan outreach event logistics
- Coordinate travel and venues for coordinator meetings
- Manage supply chain and distribution

### 🤝 Outreach
- Community engagement and awareness campaigns
- Partner with prosthetics providers and NGOs
- Develop outreach materials and templates

### 💰 Finance
- Track outreach budget and expenses
- Identify funding sources and grants
- Manage resource allocation

## Volunteer Onboarding Checklist

1. Join the GitHub organization/repository
2. Claim a task from the Issues tab
3. Complete the task and submit a PR or check it off
4. Notify your regional coordinator
