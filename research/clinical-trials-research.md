# Clinical Trials Research — Affordable Prosthetics

## Focus Areas
- Low-cost prosthetic socket fabrication
- Affordable prosthesis design for developing countries
- Expandable/rapid-fit prosthetic technologies
- 3D-printed prosthetic components

## Key Trials Identified

### 1. NCT04725461 — Low Cost Socket for Lower Limb Amputees
- **Sponsor**: Shirley Ryan AbilityLab (Chicago, IL)
- **Status**: COMPLETED
- **Summary**: Tests an investigational fabrication system using expandable rigid foam for transtibial prostheses, designed for comfort and function in developing countries.
- **Key Innovation**: Simplified socket fitting protocol — cast limb in <30 min, finish socket in 90 min.
- **Relevance**: Directly addresses affordable prosthetic access.
